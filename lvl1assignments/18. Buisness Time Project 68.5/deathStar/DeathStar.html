Presenting the Star Wars: Death Star Experience

Get ready to immerse yourself in the Star Wars universe and become a part of the epic story of the Death Star. May the Force be with you!

Join the Rebellion!

Join the Empire!

Exciting Activities

Imperial Tour: Join a guided tour led by stormtroopers and explore the massive Death Star, including the control room, detention level, and the infamous trash compactor.

X-Wing Simulator: Climb into the cockpit of a rebel X-Wing fighter and engage in high-intensity space battles against TIE Fighters. Prove your piloting skills and save the galaxy.

Droid Workshop: Assemble your very own astromech droid with the help of expert technicians. Customize its appearance and watch it come to life.

Holochess Tournament: Challenge fellow travelers to a game of Dejarik, the holographic chess-like game featured in Star Wars. Beware of the Wookiee!

Cantina Night: Experience a vibrant evening at the Death Star Cantina. Sip on exotic alien beverages, groove to live alien music, and enjoy a menu of otherworldly delights.

Lightsaber Training: Train in the art of lightsaber combat under the guidance of a Jedi Master. Put your skills to the test against a Sith Lord in a thrilling duel.

Storm the Death Star: Participate in a full-scale reenactment of the Rebel Alliance's attack on the Death Star. Join the Rebel pilots and try to hit the exhaust port!

Astromech Race: Race your newly built astromech droid through a challenging obstacle course, demonstrating your engineering and piloting abilities.

Stargazing on the Bridge: Enjoy an unforgettable evening on the Death Star's command bridge, where you can observe galaxies, planets, and stars from a unique perspective.
