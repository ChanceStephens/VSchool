Introducing the "Hogwarts Getaway" for all aspiring witches and wizards!

Unleash your inner wizard or witch and live out your dreams at Hogwarts. Enroll in the Hogwarts Getaway and let the magic begin!

Sorting Hat Ceremony: Begin your adventure with the Sorting Hat ceremony, where you'll be sorted into one of the four Hogwarts houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin.

Other Activity Options

Potions Class: Attend a Potions class and concoct potions with surprising effects. Brew Polyjuice Potion or Felix Felicis for an unforgettable experience.

Magical Dueling Tournament: Compete in the Triwizard Tournament, showcasing your magical prowess in challenges such as the Maze and the Dragon Challenge.

Yule Ball: Dress in your finest wizarding attire for the magical Yule Ball. Dance to the music of the wizarding world, surrounded by floating candles and enchanting decorations.

Hogsmeade Visit: Explore the charming village of Hogsmeade, visiting iconic locations like Honeydukes, the Three Broomsticks, and the Shrieking Shack.

Magical Creatures Parade: Join in the magical creatures parade through Hogwarts castle, where you'll encounter mystical beings from across the wizarding world.
