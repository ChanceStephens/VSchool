Black Pearl Cruise Activities

Treasure Hunts: Join our interactive treasure hunts on deserted islands, solving riddles and following cryptic maps to discover hidden pirate treasures.

Swordplay Workshops: Sharpen your cutlass skills with expert instructors, learning the art of pirate swordplay. Challenge your fellow pirates to friendly duels on deck.

Captain's Feast: Enjoy a grand feast fit for a pirate captain, featuring exotic dishes, rum tastings, and lively sea shanties around a blazing bonfire.

Nightly Pirate Ball: Dress in your finest pirate attire for the nightly Pirate Ball, where you can dance the night away under the stars, surrounded by glowing lanterns and the sound of the sea.

Swimming with Mermaids: Dive into the crystal-clear waters and swim alongside enchanting mermaids, creating memories you'll treasure forever.

Stormy Skies Show: Experience a thrilling reenactment of a pirate ship battle amid stormy seas, complete with cannon fire, fireworks, and dazzling special effects.

Visit Tortuga Port: Make a stop at the legendary pirate haven of Tortuga, where you can explore the bustling pirate market and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of this notorious island.

Pirate Movie Marathon: Relax in the ship's open-air theater and enjoy a marathon of "Pirates of the Caribbean" films, complete with popcorn and themed cocktails.
